Introduction

Welcome to Cooperation Dynamics ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website at cooperation-dynamics.pages.dev (the "Site").

Information We Collect

Our Site is a simple browser-based game that runs entirely on your device. We do not directly collect, store, or process any personal information. However, third-party services we use may collect certain information as described below.

Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our Site. Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to collect information about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our Site and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or by visiting www.aboutads.info.

Cookies

Our Site does not set any first-party cookies. However, third-party services like Google AdSense may use cookies to:

  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Measure ad performance
  • Prevent showing you the same ads repeatedly

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of advertisements on our Site.

Game Data

All game data (scores, rounds, choices) is processed locally in your browser and is not transmitted to any server or stored permanently. When you close or refresh the page, all game data is lost.

Analytics

We may use analytics services in the future to understand how visitors use our Site. If implemented, we will update this Privacy Policy accordingly.

Children's Privacy

Our Site is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Third-Party Links

Our Site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.
